DOGS TO FOSTER

In order to make the process fair to all dogs, Loyal Rescue does not provide any features to aid with filtering by breed. Breed filtering simply based on preference creates a disadvantage for the dogs that are mixed breeds.

As a foster-based rescue, we firmly believe that this process should be as open as possible for the dogs; however, Loyal rescue is not here to make promises to families that they will find the exact breed that they want.

Please do not be offended by this, as it is just a precaution to ensure that people are adopting these dogs for the right reasons.
